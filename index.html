About Us

Jenny Lee

Jenny has over 22 years experience in application development and UX/UI design. He started o.park group in 2015 to offer design and web development services to the MIT community. Previously, he worked for the MIT Skoltech Program to help create a communications strategy, build university enterprise software infrastructure, and develop custom applications for reviewing and facilitating scientific research proposals. His career at MIT began in 2001 as a design and web advisor for the Communications Office, and he was responsible for evaluating web technologies and providing technical and design recommendations. He also has experience in developing enterprise level ecommerce, recruiting, and mission critical applications for MIT, Pfizer, and Cornerstone Brands, acquired by the Home Shopping Network (HSN). He has a BA in Graphic Design and an MFA in Interactive Design from Boston University.

 

Allen Mills

Allen has worked with the o.park group team since 2016 and joined full-time in September 2022. She has a chemical engineering degree from UCONN and spent the first 12 years of her career as an air quality inspector at the Connecticut Department of Environmental Protection. She focuses on technical writing, facilitating usability testing, and market research and surveys.
